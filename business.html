If there are multiple valid outlines:

In addition to this method for writing repeated syllables, うう can also be written using long vowels. きき, くく, and ちち can all be written with two sounds, and らら, りり, るる, れれ, and ろろ can be written with the ら series.

You can use any method you'd like, so learn whichever outline is easier for you.
